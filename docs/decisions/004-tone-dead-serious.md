# Decision: Dead-Serious Combat Sports Tone

**Date:** From initial spec
**Who Decided:** Per spec
**Status:** Accepted
**Source:** tokenburner-3000-concept-v2.md, section "Tone & Copy Guide"

## The Situation
The site needs a consistent voice that makes the absurd premise work.

## What We Chose
Dead-serious combat sports organization voice. Never breaks character. Never acknowledges the absurdity. The comedy comes from the commitment.

**Use frequently:** sanctioned, professional, regulation, league-certified, competitive integrity, tale of the tape, main event, undercard, by decision, unanimous, technical knockout (TKO — "Technical Knowledge Overflow"), split decision, the commission

**Forbidden:** "haha," "lol," "just for fun," "silly," or anything that winks at the camera.

## What We Rejected
Self-aware/ironic tone that acknowledges the joke.

## Why
The bit only works if nobody breaks character. Every piece of copy, every stat label, every error message should sound like it was written by a combat sports league bureaucrat.

## Consequences
- All UI copy, error messages, loading states must be written in this voice
- Satirical sponsors are on-brand: "This fight brought to you by Unallocated Series B Capital"
- The tone guide must be shared with anyone writing copy for the project
