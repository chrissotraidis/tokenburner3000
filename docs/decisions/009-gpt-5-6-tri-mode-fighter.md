# Decision: GPT-5.6 Is One Tri-Mode Fighter

**Date:** 2026-07-11
**Who Decided:** User research plus product planning
**Status:** Accepted

## The Situation

GPT-5.6 ships as Sol, Terra, and Luna capability tiers. Three adjacent fighter
cards would crowd the roster and dilute the transformation story.

## What We Chose

Represent GPT-5.6 as one fighter that cycles between Sol, Terra, and Luna during
a fight. Each mode changes simulated speed, price, phrase behavior, and scoring
tradeoffs. Sol is the default tale-of-the-tape price.

## What We Rejected

- Three independent fighter cards
- Ignoring Terra and Luna

## Consequences

- A single signature move can drive a visually strong Sun/Earth/Moon mode shift.
- Fight records must preserve the mode timeline, not only the fighter ID.
- Cost computation must use the active mode at the time tokens are emitted.
