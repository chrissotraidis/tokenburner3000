# Decision: 5 Scoring Categories (50 Points Total)

**Date:** From initial spec
**Who Decided:** Per spec
**Status:** Accepted
**Source:** tokenburner-3000-concept-v2.md, section "4. THE SCORING"

## The Situation
Fights need a structured scoring system that's dramatic, opinionated, and funny.

## What We Chose
5 categories, each 1-10 points (50 total):
1. Verbal Devastation — Raw roast quality, wit, and burn severity
2. Theatrical Commitment — How fully they committed to the bit
3. Creative Strategy — Originality of approach, unexpected angles
4. Token Efficiency Ratio — Points per token (ironic scoring)
5. Main Character Energy — Overall presence, confidence

## What We Rejected
Not discussed. Could have been simpler (thumbs up/down) or more granular.

## Why
5 categories gives enough dimensions for interesting variation while keeping the scorecard readable. The categories themselves are funny and on-brand.

## Consequences
- Referee model must understand and apply all 5 categories consistently
- Ties are possible (same total across 5 categories)
- The "Token Efficiency Ratio" creates an ironic tension: being verbose is funny but costs points
